Frequently Asked Questions

We recommend arriving at least 2 hours before domestic and 3 hours before international departures.

Baggage allowance varies by airline and seat class. Economy allows 20kg checked baggage, Business and above allows 30–40kg.

Booking changes are subject to airline policy and may incur fees. Please contact customer service for assistance.

We accept all major credit and debit cards, as well as digital wallets.

Lounge access is available for Business and First Class passengers, and for eligible frequent flyer members.
